Our Detailed Process to SEO

Here's exactly how we plan and execute our SEO strategy for small businesses in New Jersey.

Discovery Call

We schedule a discovery to call to learn about the goals of your business and the size of your operation. We learn more about your services, which jobs or clients are most profitable to you, and what type of content we should produce.

Planning

We set up a second meeting where we can show you where you currently rank on Google, who your competitors are, and how many service pages or blogs we should do per month. We set a defined goal for the next 6-12 months.

Onboarding

Once you sign up for one of our SEO packages, we'll make a monthly plan for your content strategy, citations, and backlinks (as needed). We'll have you approve the topics every month, to make sure it aligns with your needs.

Launch

This is when we get to work. We'll send your pages to production and our writing team will write engaging, perfectly SEO'd articles that will get conversions. We will then assign the article to be reviewed and published by our editor. Finally, our on-page SEO professional will ensure the blog or page is properly optimized for search engines.
